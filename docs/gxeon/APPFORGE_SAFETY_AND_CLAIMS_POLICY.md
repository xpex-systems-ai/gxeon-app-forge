# GXEON App Forge Safety and Commercial Claims Policy

GXEON App Forge must remain manual-first, human-approved, and compliant with platform rules.

## Prohibited claims and behavior

- No guaranteed income claims.
- No fake revenue screenshots, fake testimonials, or fake reviews.
- No spam automation or mass DM automation.
- No unauthorized marketplace publishing.
- No automatic posting without user approval.
- No platform policy bypass or evasion.
- No hidden use, storage, or reuse of user tokens.

## Integration requirements

All marketplace/API integrations must start manual-first or sandbox-first. Any future assisted publishing, payment, CRM, or content distribution workflow requires explicit human approval, clear credential handling, platform-policy review, and safe rollback.
