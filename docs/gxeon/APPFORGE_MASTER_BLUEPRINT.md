# GXEON App Forge Master Blueprint

GXEON App Forge is a **Digital Product Creation OS**: a manual-first operating system for turning ideas into sellable digital products, apps, landing pages, marketplace packs, checkout blueprints, launch content, CRM follow-up, deploy evidence, and revenue tracking.

## Positioning

GXEON App Forge is not positioned as only an app builder. It preserves the existing open-source builder core from bolt.diy while adding a commercial productization layer for creators, agencies, affiliates, stores, and product launch operators.

**Core flow:** Ideia → Produto → Landing Page → App → Checkout Blueprint → Marketplace Pack → Conteúdo → CRM → Venda → Evidência.

## Major modules

- **Product Builder:** converts a business idea into offer structure, avatar, transformation promise, deliverables, and MVP scope.
- **Landing Builder:** generates landing-page copy, sections, proof blocks, FAQ, and conversion-oriented UI prompts.
- **App Builder Core:** preserves the upstream AI coding workspace for building product apps and prototypes.
- **Marketplace Pack Generator:** creates manual submission packs for platforms such as Hotmart, Kiwify, Eduzz, Monetizze, Braip, Perfect Pay, Ticto, ClickBank, Gumroad, Lemon Squeezy, Whop, AppSumo, Mercado Livre, Shopee, Amazon, Shopify, WooCommerce, and Nuvemshop.
- **Checkout Blueprint:** plans checkout structure without processing payments or creating live checkout endpoints.
- **Content Factory:** creates launch posts, email drafts, ad angles, affiliate copy, and educational content for human review.
- **CRM Inbox:** future workspace for lead/customer follow-up and evidence capture.
- **Deploy Center:** organizes deployment tasks and release checklists without hiding platform credentials.
- **Revenue Ledger:** future evidence-based tracking for sales, launches, experiments, and product performance.

## Marketplace Pack Generator concept layer

Generated fields include product title, short description, long description, SEO title, categories, tags, suggested pricing, FAQ, guarantee, delivery policy, affiliate copy, launch posts, and publication checklist. Publishing is **manual-first**. No marketplace API clients are part of this foundation.

## Checkout Blueprint concept layer

Future provider targets include Stripe, Mercado Pago, PayPal, Pix, Lemon Squeezy, Asaas, Hotmart, and Kiwify. Generated outputs include pricing model, one-time payment option, subscription plan, coupon idea, thank-you page, delivery instructions, refund/guarantee section, and checkout checklist. This module does not add SDKs, payment keys, or live checkout endpoints.

## Safety and approval rules

- Human approval is required before publishing, posting, sending messages, or submitting products.
- Marketplace publishing starts manual-first and may become sandbox-first only after review.
- No guaranteed income claims, fake earnings, spam automation, fake reviews, platform bypass, mass DM automation, or unauthorized publishing.
- User tokens must never be hidden, reused, or committed.

## GXEON ecosystem fit

GXEON App Forge is the product creation layer inside the GXEON Systems AI ecosystem. It turns AI-assisted building into a commercial operating system that packages digital products responsibly while preserving upstream attribution and MIT license obligations.
