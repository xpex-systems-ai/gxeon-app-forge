# APPFORGE-015 Hotmart Distribution OS Report

## Mission scope
GXEON App Forge now includes a manual-first Hotmart Distribution OS MVP for generating local product packages, affiliate kits, sales assets, traffic-manager briefs, launch queue items and exportable checklists.

## Manual-first strategy
The workflow is intentionally local-only. Operators generate ideas, select one package, review the draft, copy Markdown or export JSON, and then decide manually whether anything should be entered into Hotmart.

## Why auto-publishing is blocked
Auto-publishing is blocked because marketplace publication, payment activation, checkout creation, affiliate enablement and claims review require human, legal and platform-policy validation. The MVP does not include Hotmart API clients, tokens, webhook receivers, background jobs or payment processing.

## Product package workflow
1. Select niche, audience and delivery format.
2. Generate ten product ideas locally.
3. Build a Hotmart product draft with audience, problem, safe promise, offer stack, price hypothesis and manual checklist.
4. Review compliance warnings and founder approval notes.
5. Export JSON or Markdown for manual use.

## Affiliate kit workflow
The affiliate kit creates pitch copy, five ad angles, five short video hooks, WhatsApp scripts, email subject lines, a traffic-manager briefing and explicit warnings against income, sales or ROI guarantees.

## Traffic manager workflow
Traffic managers receive a brief with audience, problem, compliant angle, creative prompts and no-guarantee disclaimer. The system does not post ads, send messages or create campaigns.

## Future Hotmart API phase
A future phase may research a real Hotmart API adapter only after platform contracts, legal review, credential handling, audit logging and explicit human approval are validated. This mission does not build that integration.

## Safety guarantees
- Manual publish only.
- Local storage/export only.
- No Hotmart API calls.
- No checkout creation.
- No payment processing.
- No webhook receiver.
- No secrets, tokens or credentials.
- No database or background jobs.
- No spam or mass posting.
- No fake income claims or guaranteed-result claims.
- Founder preview and human approval required before manual publication.
