# APPFORGE-015 Hotmart Manual Publish Playbook

## Step-by-step manual publishing process
1. Export the local product package JSON and checklist Markdown.
2. Review product promise, target audience, delivery files, price and support scope.
3. Open the Hotmart dashboard manually in a browser controlled by the founder/operator.
4. Create or edit the product manually using the reviewed title, descriptions and category.
5. Upload delivery materials manually.
6. Configure pricing, refund policy and affiliate settings manually.
7. Review all claims before clicking any platform publish button.
8. Record post-publish notes outside the MVP if the founder approves publication.

## Product title checklist
- Clear product category.
- Specific audience.
- No exaggerated or guaranteed outcome claim.
- No copycat naming from another product.

## Description checklist
- Describe the problem and delivery format.
- Explain what is included in the offer stack.
- Add safe limitations and no-guarantee language.
- Remove unsupported medical, legal or financial promises.

## Pricing checklist
- Validate price hypothesis manually.
- Confirm taxes, refund policy and fees on the platform.
- Do not imply guaranteed ROI.

## Delivery checklist
- Confirm files, templates, dashboards or course modules are ready.
- Confirm access instructions and support channel.
- Confirm no secret keys or private data are included.

## Affiliate material checklist
- Provide pitch, ad angles, scripts and email subjects.
- Include prohibited-claim warnings.
- Require affiliates to avoid income, sales or ROI guarantees.

## Compliance checklist
- Search for blocked phrases such as ganho garantido, renda garantida, dinheiro fácil and lucro certo.
- Confirm all claims can be substantiated.
- Require founder approval notes before publication.

## Post-publish tracking checklist
- Record the manual publish date.
- Monitor refunds, support tickets and affiliate questions.
- Update copy only after manual review.
- Keep future API automation disabled until a separately approved phase.
