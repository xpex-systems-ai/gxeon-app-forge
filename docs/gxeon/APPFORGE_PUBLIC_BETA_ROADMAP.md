# GXEON App Forge Public Beta Roadmap

- **Beta Phase 0: Boot proof** — confirm public deployment boots and preserves the builder core.
- **Beta Phase 1: GXEON identity** — apply GXEON App Forge identity, attribution, docs, and first public shell.
- **Beta Phase 2: Product Builder MVP** — structured product idea, offer, audience, and deliverable generation.
- **Beta Phase 3: Marketplace Pack Generator MVP** — manual-first marketplace packs and publication checklists.
- **Beta Phase 4: Landing Builder commercial templates** — offer pages, waitlists, lead magnets, and launch pages.
- **Beta Phase 5: Checkout Blueprint** — checkout plans, pricing models, guarantees, delivery, and thank-you page copy.
- **Beta Phase 6: Content Factory** — launch posts, affiliate kits, ads, scripts, and email drafts for approval.
- **Beta Phase 7: CRM Inbox** — lead/customer organization and follow-up workflow concepts.
- **Beta Phase 8: Deploy Center** — deployment checklists, evidence capture, and release workflows.
- **Beta Phase 9: Revenue Ledger** — sales evidence, experiments, and product performance tracking.
- **Beta Phase 10: Founder Preview launch** — invite-only public validation with manual onboarding and safety review.
